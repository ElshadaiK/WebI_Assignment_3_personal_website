About me

I love rain. I love the sound it makes on my roof, the cold and the smell of the ground and leaves. It is my favorite weather related phenomena. Rain for many people is annoyingly constricting. I, on the other hand, am intellectually free in the rain. I soar above the clouds, twirl, dance, and frolic. I digest all that happened to me that summer then.

Sport activities: I started swimming at the age of 3. For me, it was beyond making waves in water in order to move. Swimming is where I learnt saving ones' self. It's about struggling to take a breathe in circumstances you don't think you can. Also playing basketball is my hobby, and it soon will become a profession. I have been playing for Addis Ababa University, for 8 months now.

My attitude in life is that if I want something to happen, I’ll make it happen. Though I’ve been able to take part and take lead in changing my community at a young age, I know there’s much more that I can and will do. I’ll contribute to bridge the gap of gender views in the societal construction and technology. You can see my git posts on GitHub.
I also write in my free time. My official blogging page will be official soon. Stay Tuned!
Thank you so much for reading.
