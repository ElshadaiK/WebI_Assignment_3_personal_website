The mall's entrance

Last Updated on March 29, 2020    ·    4 min read    ·    The Pull/Push entrance

At 4PM in the afternoon, I was on my way to buy a luggage. That's when I ran into a mall with a pull/push entrance. As I had to wait for my sister to accompany me, I sat down for a while and watched people enter and leave the mall. Every single person I looked into, pushed the entrance to make their way.
While I was thinking of why they all choose push, a lady got inside by pulling the entrance. I could sense the energy she applied. She even paid her time to look back after she let it close by itself. The pull instruction, in fact took much more resource; time and energy.
This speaks to me on different levels. And as I saw my sister come, I couldn't notice which instruction she took to execute. But I knew which I had to; it was time to let go of my guards, and pulled the door.

Writer's note: The entrance of that mall was a metaphoric expression of my subconscious mind, and people, of course.
